# 奠基文獻建議閱讀順序

> 不管你最後做什麼題目，以下 5 篇建議先讀。

## 必讀五篇

1. **Yin, R. K. (2018).** *Case Study Research and Applications* (6th ed.). Sage.
   → 個案研究設計的聖經，CH3 必引。

2. **Eisenhardt, K. M. (1989).** Building theories from case study research. *Academy of Management Review*, 14(4), 532-550.
   → 告訴你怎麼從個案中建構理論。被引 60,000+ 次。

3. **Teece, D. J. (2007).** Explicating dynamic capabilities: The nature and microfoundations of (sustainable) enterprise performance. *Strategic Management Journal*, 28(13), 1319-1350.
   → 動態能力的微觀基礎。被引 12,000+ 次。

4. **Sarasvathy, S. D. (2001).** Causation and effectuation: Toward a theoretical shift from economic inevitability to entrepreneurial contingency. *Academy of Management Review*, 26(2), 243-263.
   → Effectuation 理論奠基文。如果你的研究涉及策略決策，必讀。

5. **Langley, A. (1999).** Strategies for theorizing from process data. *Academy of Management Review*, 24(4), 691-710.
   → 質性過程資料分析的方法論經典。
